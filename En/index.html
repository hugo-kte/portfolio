Welcome to My Portfolio

Hi, I'm Hugo Kothe, born on September 7, 2003, in Chambray-lès-Tours. I'm passionate about technology, science, and combat sports. My journey has always been about exploring, learning, and pushing my limits.

My Journey: From Curiosity to Passion

Growing up in Véretz, a small town in Touraine, I developed two major passions: combat sports, which taught me discipline and resilience, and a fascination for how things work. I loved taking apart everyday objects just to understand their mechanisms. This curiosity naturally led me to discover electronics and, later, computer science.

In middle school, I got hooked on computer systems. I started exploring cybersecurity in my own way—bypassing school restrictions and creating small programs to entertain my friends. These early experiences built a strong, intuitive understanding of systems and networks.

In high school, I chose the NSI (Digital and Computer Science) option. It was a perfect fit and confirmed my desire to pursue a tech career. Around that time, I drew inspiration from Amy Plant's videos about École 42 and the *Mr. Robot* series, which further fueled my fascination with the digital world.

ESIEE Paris: A Key Milestone

Joining ESIEE Paris in 2019 was a game-changer. I moved to Paris with a clear goal: to study at a top school where I could dive into artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. This journey has not only sharpened my technical skills but also broadened my understanding of the professional world.

Key Experiences and Projects

Working as a kitchen assistant taught me how to handle pressure and collaborate with a team. I also got to train new employees, which helped me build strong leadership and communication skills.

As a pastry chef, I honed my craft while supervising and training new staff. I also handled sales during busy periods, improving my adaptability and multitasking abilities.

For this academic project, I developed the entire web platform, from front-end to back-end. My focus was on creating a user-friendly interface and a solid backend while integrating AI models provided by my teammates.

We built a facial recognition model using advanced computer vision techniques. This project gave me hands-on experience with facial encoding challenges and strengthened my applied AI knowledge.

Looking Ahead

One of my biggest passions is the automotive industry. I dream of contributing to innovative AI and cybersecurity solutions for this field. My goal is to merge my technical skills with an industry that's constantly evolving and shaping the future.
